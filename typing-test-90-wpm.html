Is 90 WPM a Good Typing Speed?

Yes, 90 WPM is an excellent typing speed. It is far above average and considered advanced for most jobs, school tasks, and professional communication.

Typing Speed Levels

  • 20 to 30 WPM: beginner
  • 40 WPM: average
  • 50 to 60 WPM: good
  • 70 WPM: strong
  • 80 WPM: advanced
  • 90 WPM: elite everyday typing speed

Why Practice for 90 WPM?

  • Work faster with fewer interruptions
  • Improve productivity for typing-heavy tasks
  • Prepare for advanced job requirements
  • Build stronger typing endurance

How to Reach 90 WPM

To reach 90 WPM, focus on building clean technique first. Speed improves faster when your typing is accurate and relaxed.

  • Practice daily for 15 to 20 minutes
  • Focus on 95%+ accuracy
  • Use short tests to improve burst speed
  • Use longer tests to build endurance
  • Review weak words and key patterns

Accuracy Still Matters

Typing 90 WPM is only useful if your accuracy stays high. If errors increase too much, your effective typing speed drops.

  • Aim for 95% to 98% accuracy
  • Slow slightly if mistakes increase
  • Repeat practice to improve control
  • Build rhythm instead of rushing

How Long Does It Take to Reach 90 WPM?

That depends on your current level and practice consistency. If you already type 60 to 80 WPM, focused daily training can help you improve over time.

Progress is not instant. The best results come from steady practice and tracking your improvement.
