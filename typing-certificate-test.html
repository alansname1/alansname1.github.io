Typing Speed Test with Certificate

This typing speed test allows you to measure your typing speed (WPM) and track your accuracy. It can help you prepare for jobs that require typing skills.

Why Get a Typing Certificate?

A typing certificate can help demonstrate your typing speed and accuracy to employers. Many data entry and remote jobs require proof of typing ability.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

A good typing speed is usually between 40–60 WPM. Advanced typists can reach 70–100 WPM with high accuracy.

How to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice daily with typing tests
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use proper finger placement
  • Track your progress over time
