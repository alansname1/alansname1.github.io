How Fast Can People Type?

Most people type around 40 WPM, while professional typists often type 65–100 WPM. Elite typists can go much faster.

What Helps Top Typists Type Faster?

  • Daily practice
  • Strong muscle memory
  • High accuracy
  • Proper finger placement

Can You Improve Your Typing Speed?

Yes. Regular typing practice can help most people improve speed and accuracy over time.
