Why Practice with a 1 Minute Typing Test?

A 1 minute typing test gives you a quick and repeatable way to measure your typing performance. Because it only takes 60 seconds, it is easy to practice multiple times per day and track your progress.

Short tests are especially helpful for building habits and improving accuracy without feeling overwhelmed.

What This Typing Test Measures

  • Words Per Minute (WPM): your typing speed
  • Accuracy: how correctly you type the text
  • Character Count: total characters typed

Improving both speed and accuracy leads to better real-world typing performance.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

  • 20–30 WPM: Beginner
  • 40 WPM: Average
  • 50–60 WPM: Good
  • 60–80 WPM: Strong
  • 80+ WPM: Advanced

How to Improve Typing Speed

  • Practice daily
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use correct finger placement
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
