Typing Test for Office Jobs

This typing test for office jobs helps improve typing speed, accuracy, and efficiency for administrative and workplace tasks.

Why Typing Skills Matter in Office Jobs

Office jobs require typing for emails, documents, and data entry. Faster typing increases productivity and efficiency.

What Is a Good Typing Speed for Office Work?

Most office jobs require 40–60 WPM. Advanced roles may require 70+ WPM with high accuracy.

How to Improve Typing Speed for Office Work

  • Practice typing daily
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use typing tests regularly
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard

Best Way to Practice

Timed typing tests simulate real office tasks and help improve speed and consistency.
