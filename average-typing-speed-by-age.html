Average Typing Speed by Age

Typing speed is usually measured in words per minute (WPM). The average typing speed can vary depending on age, experience, and how often someone uses a computer.

Average Typing Speed Chart

Age Group Average Typing Speed
Children (8–12) 15–25 WPM
Teenagers 30–40 WPM
Adults 40–50 WPM
Office Professionals 50–65 WPM
Fast Typists 65–80 WPM
Expert Typists 80+ WPM

What Is Considered a Good Typing Speed?

For most people, a typing speed of around 40 words per minute is considered average. Many office jobs prefer typing speeds of 50 to 60 WPM or higher.

How to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice typing regularly
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Learn correct keyboard finger placement
  • Use timed typing tests
  • Practice with sentences and paragraphs
