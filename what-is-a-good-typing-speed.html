What Is a Good Typing Speed?

A good typing speed depends on your goals, but most people type around 40 words per minute (WPM). With practice, many people improve to 60–80 WPM or higher.

Average Typing Speeds

  • Beginner: 20–40 WPM
  • Average: 40–60 WPM
  • Advanced: 60–80 WPM
  • Expert: 80+ WPM

What Typing Speed Do Jobs Require?

Most jobs require at least 40–60 WPM. Data entry and administrative roles may require higher speeds with strong accuracy.

Why Typing Speed Matters

Faster typing helps improve productivity, reduce time spent on tasks, and is important for school, work, and online communication.

How to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice typing every day
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use timed typing tests
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
