Typing Speed Goals (WPM Guide)

Understanding typing speed goals can help you track progress and improve faster. Whether you are a beginner or advanced typist, setting clear WPM targets is key.

Typing Speed Goals by Level

  • Beginner: 20–30 WPM
  • Average: 40 WPM
  • Intermediate: 50–60 WPM
  • Advanced: 70–80 WPM
  • Professional: 90–100+ WPM

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

A good typing speed depends on your goals. For school and everyday use, 40–60 WPM is sufficient. For jobs such as data entry or customer service, 50–70 WPM is often required.

How to Reach Your Typing Speed Goals

  • Practice typing daily
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Use timed typing tests
  • Track your progress over time

How Long Does It Take to Improve?

With consistent daily practice, most people can increase their typing speed within a few weeks. Small improvements each day lead to significant long-term results.

Typing Tips for Faster Results

  • Keep your hands in proper position
  • Don’t rush — accuracy builds speed
  • Practice in short, consistent sessions
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
