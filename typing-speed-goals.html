What Is a Good Typing Speed?

Most people type between 35–45 WPM.

👉 Can you beat that?

Typing speed is measured in words per minute (WPM). Knowing your typing speed helps you understand your level and set realistic goals for improvement.

Typing Speed Levels (WPM)

  • Beginner: 20–30 WPM
  • Average: 35–45 WPM
  • Intermediate: 50–60 WPM
  • Advanced: 70–80 WPM
  • Professional: 90–100+ WPM

What Typing Speed Should You Aim For?

If you're just starting, aim for 40 WPM. If you're preparing for jobs, aim for 50–70 WPM. If you want to stand out, aim for 80+ WPM.

How Fast Can You Improve?

Most people improve faster than they expect. With daily practice, it's common to increase typing speed within a few weeks.

Simple Plan to Increase Your WPM

  • Practice 5–10 minutes per day
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use timed typing tests
  • Track your progress
