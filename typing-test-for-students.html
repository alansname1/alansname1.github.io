Why Typing Skills Matter for Students

Students use typing for assignments, research, and communication. Faster typing helps save time, improve productivity, and reduce frustration.

What Is a Good Typing Speed for Students?

  • Beginner: 20–30 WPM
  • Intermediate: 40–60 WPM
  • Advanced: 60+ WPM

How Students Can Improve Typing Speed

  • Practice typing daily
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Use timed typing tests
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
