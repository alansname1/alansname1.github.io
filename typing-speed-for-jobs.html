Typing Speed for Jobs

Many jobs require a minimum typing speed, especially roles in data entry, customer service, and administrative work. A typical requirement is between 40 and 60 words per minute.

Improving your typing speed can increase productivity and make you more competitive in the job market. Accuracy is just as important as speed, so focus on both when practicing.

Using free typing tests regularly can help you track your progress and prepare for job requirements.
