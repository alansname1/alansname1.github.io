Our Purpose

Free Typing Test Online was created to provide simple typing tests and educational typing resources for people who want to improve practical keyboard skills.

Typing is an important skill for students, job seekers, office workers, remote workers, writers, and anyone who uses a computer regularly. This website focuses on clear explanations, beginner-friendly practice, and useful typing improvement guides.

Many typing websites focus only on speed scores. This site also focuses on accuracy, consistency, touch typing habits, keyboard familiarity, and practical typing skills that can be useful in school, work, and everyday computer use.

Who This Website Is For

This website is designed for people who want practical typing help without complicated instructions. It can be useful for beginners, students, adults, seniors, job seekers, remote workers, and everyday computer users.

The goal is to make typing practice easier to understand and easier to use. Visitors can check typing speed, measure accuracy, and learn better typing habits over time.

About the Creator

My name is Alan, and I created Free Typing Test Online to provide free typing tests, typing practice resources, and educational guides for students, job seekers, remote workers, seniors, and everyday computer users.

The goal of this website is to make typing practice accessible and easy to understand. The guides and resources on this site are regularly reviewed and updated to improve clarity, usability, and educational value.

This website is intended to provide practical information about typing speed, typing accuracy, touch typing techniques, and keyboard skills through easy-to-follow guides and typing practice resources.

Our Approach

The content on this website is written to be clear, practical, and educational. Pages are organized around typing tests, typing accuracy, touch typing, beginner practice, and general keyboard improvement.

We avoid unrealistic promises. Typing improvement takes practice and consistency. The resources on this website are intended to help users build skill gradually over time.

Whether you are preparing for school, office work, remote work, data entry, customer service, or simply want to type more comfortably, the goal is to provide useful information that supports steady improvement.
