60 WPM Typing Test

A 60 WPM typing test helps you determine whether you can type at an above-average speed. Typing at 60 words per minute is considered a strong skill and is often required for many jobs.

If you can reach 60 WPM with good accuracy, you have a reliable typing ability that can improve productivity, reduce workload time, and support a wide range of computer-based tasks.

Is 60 WPM a Good Typing Speed?

Yes, 60 WPM is considered a good typing speed. It is well above average and shows that you have solid keyboard skills, strong accuracy, and good typing rhythm.

  • Above average typing performance
  • Useful for many professional jobs
  • Improves productivity and efficiency

Who Needs 60 WPM?

Many roles benefit from or require a typing speed of around 60 WPM, especially jobs that involve regular computer use and communication.

  • Students and writers

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

Typing speed is measured in words per minute (WPM). While 40 WPM is considered average, reaching 60 WPM places you above average and shows strong typing ability.

Many people aim for 60 WPM because it balances speed and accuracy, making typing more efficient for both work and everyday tasks.

Practice Daily to Improve

The best way to reach 60 WPM is through consistent practice. Even short sessions of 10 to 15 minutes per day can help improve typing speed, accuracy, and confidence.
