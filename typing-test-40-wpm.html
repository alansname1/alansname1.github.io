What Is a Good Typing Speed?

Typing speed is measured in words per minute (WPM). While 40 WPM is average, many people aim for 50 WPM to 60 WPM or higher to improve productivity and typing efficiency.

Improving beyond 40 WPM takes consistent practice and attention to accuracy. Reducing mistakes is one of the fastest ways to increase your overall typing speed.

Practice Daily to Improve

The best way to increase your typing speed is through daily practice. Even short sessions of 10 to 15 minutes can help you build muscle memory, improve accuracy, and type more confidently.
