Typing Speed Calculator (WPM)

Typing speed is measured in words per minute (WPM). A standard word is considered five characters including spaces.

How Typing Speed Is Calculated

Typing speed is calculated using this formula:

Characters typed ÷ 5 = words typed

Words typed ÷ time in minutes = WPM

Example

If you type 250 characters in one minute:

250 ÷ 5 = 50 words per minute (50 WPM)

Average Typing Speed

  • Beginner: 20–30 WPM
  • Average typist: 40–50 WPM
  • Office professional: 50–65 WPM
  • Advanced typist: 65–80 WPM
  • Expert typist: 80+ WPM
