Typing Speed Chart

Typing speed is usually measured in words per minute, often called WPM. The average typing speed for most adults is around forty words per minute.

Average Typing Speeds

  • Beginner: 20–30 WPM
  • Average: 40 WPM
  • Advanced: 60–80 WPM
  • Professional: 100+ WPM

Regular typing practice helps increase both speed and accuracy.
