Why Practice With a 5 Minute Typing Test?

The 5 minute typing test is ideal for measuring endurance and consistency over longer typing sessions. It helps simulate real-world typing conditions used in jobs, office tasks, and online assessments. It is also useful for spotting when your accuracy drops as your hands get tired.

What Is a Good Score on a 5 Minute Typing Test?

Many people type around 40 words per minute on average. A score above that can be a solid sign of progress, especially when your accuracy stays high. For office work, data entry, and other typing-heavy tasks, both speed and accuracy matter.

How Often Should You Practice?

Short daily sessions can help, but using a 5 minute typing test a few times each week is also a strong way to build rhythm and endurance. The more consistently you practice, the more natural the keyboard becomes.
