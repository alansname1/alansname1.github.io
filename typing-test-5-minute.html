Why Use a 5 Minute Typing Test?

A 5 minute typing test is ideal for measuring real typing performance. Short tests can give fast results, but longer tests show how well you maintain speed and accuracy over time.

This type of test is especially useful for job preparation, since many real-world tasks require sustained typing rather than quick bursts.

What Does This Test Measure?

  • WPM: Your typing speed in words per minute
  • Accuracy: How correctly you type the given text
  • Consistency: How well you maintain performance over time

These metrics together give a much clearer picture of your actual typing ability.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

The average typing speed is around 40 WPM. A good typing speed is usually 50 to 60 WPM, while 70 WPM and above is considered strong.

If you are below average, focus on improving accuracy first. Speed will increase naturally over time.

Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice regularly with longer tests
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Use proper finger placement
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
  • Track your progress over time
