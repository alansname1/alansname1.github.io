Typing Test for 45 WPM

A typing test for 45 WPM is a useful goal for people who want to type faster than average. Since average typing speed is often around 40 words per minute, reaching 45 WPM shows steady improvement and better keyboard control.

This typing level can be helpful for students, office workers, and anyone who wants to type more efficiently during daily computer use.

Is 45 WPM a Good Typing Speed?

Yes, 45 WPM is a good typing speed for most everyday needs. It is slightly above average and can make writing, emailing, and online work much easier.

  • Faster than average typing speed
  • Useful for work and school tasks
  • Strong base for improving toward 50 WPM and beyond
