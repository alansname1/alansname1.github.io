Typing Speed Levels

Typing speed levels help you understand how your words per minute (WPM) compares from beginner to expert typists.

Typing Speed Levels

  • 10–20 WPM: Beginner
  • 20–40 WPM: Basic
  • 40–60 WPM: Average
  • 60–80 WPM: Advanced
  • 80–100 WPM: Fast
  • 100+ WPM: Expert

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

A good typing speed is usually around 40–70 WPM. Many office workers and students type in this range.

How to Move Up a Typing Level

  • Practice every day
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Use timed typing tests
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard

Why Typing Speed Matters

Faster typing can improve productivity, save time, and help with school, work, and online tasks.
