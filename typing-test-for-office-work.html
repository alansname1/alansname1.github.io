This typing test for office work helps improve typing speed, accuracy, and confidence for everyday workplace tasks.

Why Typing Matters in Office Work

Office work often includes emails, reports, spreadsheets, and data entry. Faster typing helps you finish tasks more efficiently.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

Most office jobs require around 40 to 60 words per minute, while advanced roles may need even more.

How to Improve

  • Practice daily
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use timed typing tests
