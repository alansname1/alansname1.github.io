Is 70 WPM a Good Typing Speed?

Yes, 70 WPM is a very good typing speed. It is well above average and shows strong typing control, better efficiency, and advanced keyboard confidence.

Many professional typists, writers, students, and office workers aim for 70 WPM because it makes typing tasks feel faster and more natural. Reaching this level can also help prepare you for even higher goals like 80 WPM and beyond.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

Typing speed is measured in words per minute (WPM). While 40 WPM is considered average and 50 to 60 WPM is considered good, 70 WPM places you in a strong typing category.

At this level, accuracy becomes even more important. Typing fast while making too many mistakes can slow you down overall, so accurate typing should remain a top priority.

Practice Daily to Improve

The best way to improve typing speed is through consistent daily practice. Even 10 to 15 minutes per day can help you build endurance, increase speed, and improve typing confidence over time.
