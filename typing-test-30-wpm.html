What Is a Good Typing Speed?

Typing speed is measured in words per minute (WPM). Most people type around 40 WPM, while beginners often start lower. Reaching 30 WPM is a strong milestone that shows steady improvement and better keyboard control.

If your goal is to go beyond 30 WPM, consistent practice and accuracy will help you improve over time. Once you're comfortable at 30 WPM, try working toward 35 WPM or 40 WPM.

Practice Daily to Improve

The best way to improve typing speed is to practice consistently. Even 10 to 15 minutes per day can help you increase speed, reduce mistakes, and build confidence.
