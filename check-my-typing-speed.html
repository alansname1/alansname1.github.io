Check My Typing Speed

If you want to check your typing speed, using a free typing test is the easiest way to measure your words per minute (WPM) and accuracy.

How to Check Your Typing Speed

Typing tests measure how many words you can type per minute and how accurate your typing is.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

The average typing speed is around 40 WPM. Many people improve to 60–80 WPM with practice.

How to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice typing every day
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Use timed typing tests
  • Track your progress regularly

Why Track Your Typing Speed?

Tracking your typing speed helps you see improvement over time and stay motivated.
