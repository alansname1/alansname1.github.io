How Fast Should I Type?

Typing speed is measured in words per minute (WPM). The average typing speed for most adults is around 40 WPM. Many office workers type between 50 and 70 WPM, while professional typists may reach speeds of 80 to 100 WPM or higher.

Average Typing Speed Levels

  • Beginner: 20–30 WPM
  • Average: 40 WPM
  • Office Professional: 50–70 WPM
  • Advanced Typist: 80–100 WPM
