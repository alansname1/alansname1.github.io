Typing Test Accuracy Percentage

Typing test accuracy percentage measures how correctly you type compared to the total number of characters. It is just as important as typing speed.

What Is a Good Typing Accuracy Percentage?

A good typing accuracy is usually 90% or higher. Professional typists often reach 95–100% accuracy.

Why Accuracy Matters

High accuracy reduces errors, saves time, and improves overall typing performance in school and work.

How to Improve Typing Accuracy

  • Slow down and focus on correct typing
  • Practice regularly
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
  • Use typing tests with accuracy tracking

Accuracy vs Speed

It is better to type accurately first, then increase speed. Speed improves naturally as accuracy improves.
