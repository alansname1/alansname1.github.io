Typing Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good typing speed for jobs?

Most jobs require between 40 and 70 words per minute (WPM). Advanced roles may require higher speeds with strong accuracy.

How can I pass a typing test for a job?

Practice regularly, focus on accuracy, and use timed typing tests to build speed and confidence.

Do employers require typing tests?

Yes, many employers require typing tests for roles such as data entry, customer service, and administrative positions.

How long should I practice typing daily?

Practicing 10–20 minutes per day can significantly improve typing speed over time.
