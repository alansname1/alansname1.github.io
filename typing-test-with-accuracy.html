Why Accuracy Matters in Typing

Typing speed is important, but accuracy is just as critical. Mistakes can slow you down and reduce productivity.

What Is a Good Typing Accuracy?

A good typing accuracy is typically 90% or higher. Professional typists often reach 95–100% accuracy.

How to Improve Typing Accuracy

  • Slow down and focus on correct typing
  • Practice regularly
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
  • Use typing tests with accuracy tracking

Best Way to Practice

Timed typing tests that track both speed and accuracy help you improve faster.
