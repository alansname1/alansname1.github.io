Is 80 WPM a Good Typing Speed?

Yes, 80 WPM is a very good typing speed. It is well above average and is considered strong for most office, school, and professional tasks. Many people never reach 80 WPM, so achieving this level shows strong typing skill and good hand coordination.

Typing Speed Levels

  • 20 to 30 WPM: beginner
  • 40 WPM: average
  • 50 to 60 WPM: good
  • 60 to 70 WPM: strong
  • 80 WPM: advanced and highly efficient

Why Practice for 80 WPM?

Practicing for 80 WPM can improve your daily productivity and make typing-heavy tasks easier. It can also help if you want to work faster in school, office jobs, remote jobs, customer support, or other roles that require written communication.

  • Work faster with fewer pauses
  • Handle typing-heavy tasks more comfortably
  • Improve confidence in job-related typing tests
  • Build stronger typing endurance and consistency

How to Reach 80 WPM

Most people do not reach 80 WPM by rushing. The best approach is to build strong accuracy first, then increase speed without losing control.

  • Practice daily for 10 to 20 minutes
  • Focus on typing cleanly before typing faster
  • Use short typing tests for speed checks
  • Use longer typing tests to build endurance
  • Track your WPM and accuracy weekly

Accuracy Still Matters at 80 WPM

Typing 80 WPM is impressive, but accuracy still matters. Fast typing with many mistakes is less useful than slightly slower typing with strong accuracy. If you want to truly perform at 80 WPM, aim to keep your typing accuracy high while maintaining speed.

  • Aim for 95% accuracy or higher
  • Slow down slightly if errors increase
  • Repeat practice sessions to build control
  • Focus on hard words and weak key combinations

How Long Does It Take to Reach 80 WPM?

The time it takes depends on your current level. If you already type around 50 to 60 WPM, consistent daily practice can help you move higher over time. If you are a beginner, it may take longer, but steady practice still works.

The key is consistency. Small improvements each week can lead to big gains over time.
