Typing WPM Test Online

This typing WPM test online helps you measure your words per minute and track your typing improvement.

What Is WPM?

WPM means words per minute and is the standard way to measure typing speed.

What Is a Good WPM?

  • Beginner: 20–40 WPM
  • Average: 40–60 WPM
  • Advanced: 60–80 WPM
  • Expert: 80+ WPM

How to Improve Your WPM

  • Practice every day
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use timed typing tests regularly
