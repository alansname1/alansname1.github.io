Average Typing Speed

The average typing speed for most people is around 40 words per minute (WPM). With practice, many people improve to 60–80 WPM or higher.

Average Typing Speed by Skill Level

  • Beginner: 20–40 WPM
  • Intermediate: 40–60 WPM
  • Advanced: 60–80 WPM
  • Expert: 80+ WPM

Why Typing Speed Matters

Typing faster helps you complete tasks more efficiently, improves productivity, and is important for many jobs.

How to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice typing every day
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Use typing tests regularly
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
