Typing Practice for Seniors

This page helps seniors improve their typing speed and accuracy with simple typing exercises.

Practicing typing regularly can help older adults become more comfortable using computers, sending emails, and working online.

Tips to Improve Typing

  • Practice typing for 5 minutes each day
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Use all fingers instead of just two
  • Take breaks to avoid hand strain
