Why Daily Typing Practice Works

Typing is a skill built through repetition and muscle memory. Practicing daily helps your fingers move faster and more accurately without thinking.

10 Minute Daily Typing Routine

  • 2 minutes: Warm up with easy typing
  • 5 minutes: Timed typing test
  • 3 minutes: Focus on accuracy

How to Improve Faster

  • Practice every day (consistency beats long sessions)
  • Focus on accuracy first, then speed
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
  • Track your WPM progress

What Results to Expect

  • Week 1: noticeable improvement in accuracy
  • Week 2–3: faster typing speed
  • 1 Month: strong improvement in WPM

Recommended Typing Speeds

  • Beginner: 20–30 WPM
  • Average: 40 WPM
  • Advanced: 60–80 WPM
  • Professional: 100+ WPM
