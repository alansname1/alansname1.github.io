Typing WPM Chart

This typing WPM chart shows how your words per minute compares to common typing skill levels.

Typing WPM Levels

  • 20–40 WPM: Beginner
  • 40–60 WPM: Average
  • 60–80 WPM: Advanced
  • 80–100 WPM: Professional
  • 100+ WPM: Expert

Why WPM Matters

Higher typing speed can improve productivity, save time, and help with school, office work, and data entry jobs.

How to Improve Your WPM

  • Practice every day
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use timed typing tests
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
