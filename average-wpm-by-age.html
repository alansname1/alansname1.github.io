Average WPM by Age

Average WPM by age can vary depending on typing experience, school, work, and daily computer use. Comparing your typing speed can help you see where you stand.

Average WPM by Age Group

  • Children: 10–30 WPM
  • Teens: 30–50 WPM
  • Adults: 40–70 WPM
  • Older adults: 30–60 WPM

What Affects WPM?

Typing speed depends on practice, keyboard familiarity, and how often you use a computer. People who type every day usually improve faster.

How to Increase Your WPM

  • Practice typing daily
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use timed typing tests
  • Build consistency over time

How to Measure Your WPM

The easiest way to measure WPM is to take a timed typing test and compare your results over time.
