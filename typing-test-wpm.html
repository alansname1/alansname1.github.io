Typing Test WPM

This typing test WPM helps you measure your typing speed in words per minute and improve your overall typing performance.

What Does WPM Mean?

WPM stands for words per minute. It measures how fast you can type and is the most common way to evaluate typing speed.

What Is a Good WPM?

The average typing speed is about 40 WPM. Many professionals type between 60–80 WPM, while expert typists can reach 100+ WPM.

How to Improve Your WPM

  • Practice typing every day
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Use typing tests regularly
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard

Why WPM Matters

Higher WPM allows you to complete work faster, improve productivity, and perform better in typing-related jobs.
