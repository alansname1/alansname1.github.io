Editorial Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Free Typing Test Online is committed to providing clear, accurate, and practical educational content that helps visitors improve their typing speed, typing accuracy, and keyboard skills.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make typing practice simple, free, and accessible for students, job seekers, professionals, beginners, and anyone wanting to improve keyboard skills.

How Content Is Created

Articles are researched, written, and reviewed before publication. Content is created to educate readers using practical advice, real-world examples, and commonly accepted typing techniques.

Accuracy

We make every reasonable effort to publish accurate information. If errors are discovered, corrections and updates are made whenever appropriate.

Content Updates

Existing articles are reviewed periodically to keep information current, useful, and relevant.

Editorial Independence

Content decisions are based on educational value and user benefit. We do not publish articles simply to promote products or services.

Feedback

If you notice an error or have suggestions for improving our content, please visit our Contact Page. We appreciate constructive feedback from our readers.
