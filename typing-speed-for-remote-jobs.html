Typing Speed for Remote Jobs

Typing speed for remote jobs can make a big difference in productivity and job performance.

Recommended Speed

Many remote jobs prefer at least 40 to 60 WPM, with some roles needing more.

Why It Matters

Remote work often depends on email, chat, forms, and online communication.

How to Improve

  • Practice every day
  • Use short and long typing tests
  • Focus on accuracy
