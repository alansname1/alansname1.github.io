Why Typing Matters in Customer Service

Customer service work depends heavily on communication. Faster typing allows you to respond quickly, reduce wait times, and improve customer satisfaction.

Good Typing Speed for Customer Support

  • Minimum: 40 WPM
  • Preferred: 50–60 WPM
  • Advanced: 60+ WPM

How to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice daily with typing tests
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Use timed tests to build speed
  • Practice real sentences (not random words)
