Typing Speed Benchmarks

  • 20–30 WPM: Beginner
  • 40 WPM: Average
  • 50–60 WPM: Good
  • 60–80 WPM: Strong
  • 80+ WPM: Advanced

How to Improve Typing Speed

  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Practice daily
  • Use proper finger placement
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
