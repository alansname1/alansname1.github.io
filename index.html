Typing Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good typing speed?

The average typing speed is around 40 words per minute (WPM). Professional typists can reach 65–100 WPM.

How can I improve typing speed?

Practice daily, focus on accuracy, and use timed typing tests to track your progress.

How long does it take to improve?

With consistent practice, most people improve within a few weeks.
