What Is a Good Typing Speed?

A common average typing speed is around 40 words per minute, but job-related typing goals vary by role. Entry-level office jobs may look for 35 to 45 WPM, while data entry and transcription roles may prefer faster speeds and higher accuracy.

How to Improve Typing Speed

The best way to improve typing is to practice consistently and focus on accuracy before pushing for more speed. When you reduce mistakes and build better keyboard habits, your typing speed usually improves naturally over time.

  • Practice typing a little every day
  • Use timed typing tests to track progress
  • Focus on clean typing before trying to go faster
  • Practice longer tests when preparing for work-related typing tasks

Typing Practice for Jobs and Real-World Use

Strong typing skills can help with office work, administrative tasks, data entry, online customer service, and many remote jobs. If you are practicing for employment, longer tests and role-specific pages can help you prepare more effectively.
