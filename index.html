Why Typing Skills Matter

Typing is one of the most useful digital skills today. Strong typing ability can help students finish assignments faster, professionals complete tasks more efficiently, and job seekers prepare for typing-based hiring tests.

Average adult typing speed: around 40 WPM.

Good typing speed: 50 to 60 WPM.

Advanced typing speed: 80+ WPM.

With regular practice, many users improve faster than expected.
